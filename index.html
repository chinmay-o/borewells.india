about us

Responsibly fulfilling your basic necessity, water.

Founded in 1980s, Borewells India was the first borewell company in North Malabar. We have redefined and revolutionized the borewell industry with cutting-edge technology and unparalleled service over the years. We have played an instrumental role in popularising borewells and dispelling myths around borewells and borewell drilling. Our experience and value driven approach helps us complete every project punctually and responsibly, with the aid of state-of-the-art technology, complying with safety protocols and government guidelines.

Our Services

Borewell
Drilling.
We use state-of-the-art technology for borewell drilling which ensures borewell longevity, quality and safety.
Borewell
Flushing.
Borewell flushing helps in unclogging or unblocking your already installed borewell, high pressure air is blown into borewell to decongest it.
Water Pump
Installation.
We install water pumps of any size and configurations, as per your need. We provide this service for both industrial and residential requirements.
